Quick Assists

Answers to our most frequently asked questions are just one click away.

Returns & Exchanges

What is Demon Slayer's return policy?

Demon Slayer accepts returns within 30 days of purchase. The product must be unused and in its original condition with tags attached.

How do I return or exchange my order?

To return or exchange, please visit our Returns page or contact our customer support team.

Where is my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Please check your bank or payment provider for updates.

Shipping & Delivery

What are the shipping options?

We offer standard and expedited shipping options, available at checkout.

Can I pick up in store?

Currently, in-store pickup is not available. All orders are shipped directly to your address.

How do I get free shipping?

Free shipping is available for orders over $100. Look for promotions offering free shipping at checkout.

Orders & Payment

Where is my order?

Track your order status by visiting the "Order Tracking" page or checking your confirmation email.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can be canceled or changed within 1 hour of placing the order. Please contact our support team immediately.

What are the payment options?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and other payment methods supported by our checkout system.

Membership & Apps

What is Membership?

Membership gives you access to exclusive discounts, early product releases, and special events.

What is Special Access?

Special Access allows you to purchase limited-edition items and participate in special events.

How do I join events?

You can join events by signing up on our website. Check our Events page for more details.

Company Info

Do products have a warranty?

Yes, most products come with a 1-year warranty. Please check the product description for warranty details.

Can I recycle items?

We accept product returns for recycling. Please visit our Recycling page for more information.

Do you offer grants or donations?

Yes, we have a foundation that provides grants for community projects. Learn more on our "Giving Back" page.

Shopping

How do I find the right size?

Please check our size guide on each product page to find the best fit for your measurements.

How can I get the best deals?

Sign up for our newsletter to get exclusive discounts, or check our "Sales" section for limited-time offers.

Do you offer product advice?

Yes, our customer service team is happy to help you find the right products. You can reach out through our contact page.
